Publications

Researching AI as a smarter layer for cybersecurity defense.

My published paper explores how AI and machine learning help detect, predict, and respond to modern security threats faster than purely signature-based approaches.

ICIRDST-2025

AI in Cybersecurity: A Smart Approach to Threat Mitigation

Authors: Aditya Dinesh Bandal, Geetika Narang, and Rupali Maske from the Department of Computer Engineering, Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Conference
International Conference on Innovations and Research Directions in Science and Technology
ISBN
978-93-344-0835-5
Keywords
AI, cybersecurity, threat mitigation, machine learning, intrusion detection, anomaly detection
Open Research Paper

Abstract Focus

What the paper covers

The paper discusses how AI-enabled systems can identify zero-day vulnerabilities, detect subtle patterns of malicious behavior, automate incident response, and strengthen intrusion detection, malware analysis, phishing detection, and predictive threat hunting. It also covers challenges such as adversarial AI and explainable AI in security operations.
